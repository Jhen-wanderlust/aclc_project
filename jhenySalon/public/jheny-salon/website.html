Salon App Objectives

* To help customer finds the nearest salons.

* To provide automated way of booking process and manage the time availability of staffs.

* To enhance the market of the salon services through posting rewards and promos online.

* To have fast data processing and achieve more customers as their salon will be visible on the search engine.

* To help users make an appointment on the go.

* To automate the manual reservation of salon.
